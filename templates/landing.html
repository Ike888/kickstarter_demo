Kickstarter Success

Using classification techniques, Kickstarter Success can help predict how successful a kickstarter campaign will be based on the monetary goal, description, campaign length, and catagories.

Will your kickstarter succeed?

Based upon a set of parameters, Kickstarter Success will be able to predict with a 76% accuracy rate of whether your campaign will be successful. Our model is a gradient boost classification model using gridsearch to tune the hyperparameters.
